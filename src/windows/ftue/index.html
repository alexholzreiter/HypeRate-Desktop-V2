Welcome to HypeRate Desktop

Show your live heart rate in-game. Connect your HypeRate session and your BPM appears as a floating overlay over any game.

1
Open HypeRate Start the HypeRate app on your phone or PC and begin a session to get your Session ID.
2
Enter your Session ID Paste your Session ID into the Connection field and click Connect.
3
Launch the overlay Click Show Overlay — then drag it anywhere on screen. Your BPM will appear live.
4
Hotkey Use Ctrl+Shift+H to quickly show or hide the overlay while in-game.
Launch at startup Automatically open HypeRate Desktop when your computer starts.
